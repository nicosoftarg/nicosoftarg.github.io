PRIVACY POLICY

NicoSoftArg is committed to protecting the privacy of its users, especially children.

1. Information for Children Under 13

Arcade World Cups is designed to be safe for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect, solicit, or store any personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13.

2. Data Collection

Our game does not use registration systems, user profiles, or chat features that require personal data. We only collect anonymous technical data (device model, OS version) to improve game performance.

3. Advertising and External Links

The game may contain links to this website or the app store. We do not show behaviorally targeted ads to users identified as minors.

4. Privacy Contact

If you believe we have accidentally collected information from a minor, please contact us immediately through our contact form.
